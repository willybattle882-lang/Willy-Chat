Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Anonymous by design

No email, no username. Your photo is your identity. Messages are not linked to any personal data.

Profile code

Your 6-digit code is the only way to recover your profile. We do not store any information that links the code to your identity.

Hall of Fame

Photos added to the Hall of Fame are publicly visible to anyone visiting the site. You must explicitly consent before your photo is added. You can request removal directly from your stats page.

Consent & removal

You must be 18+. You can delete your profile and photo at any time from your stats page.
